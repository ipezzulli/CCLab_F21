LEATHER

Whilst not a woven textile, Leather is considered by many to be the very first fabric. Leather is a durable and flexible material created by tanning animal rawhides. Animal hides were first recognized in prehistoric times when early man began to use forms of leather for clothing and shelter against harsh conditions. Nowadays, leather is used in practical applications like motorcycle clothing, along with fashion pieces like jackets.


Common items used with this: Jackets, pants, shoes, belts, handbags
