ORGANIC COTTON

Organic cotton is grown using methods which have a low impact on the environment, ses non-genetically modified plants, and without the use of any synthetic agricultural chemicals such as fertilizers or pesticide. Although organic cotton prices are higher than the traditional equivalent, the by-products are cleaner air, conserved water, purer soil and less toxic chemicals being circulated through the local water systems.
