WOOL

Wool is one the oldest fibers known to man and was one of the first fibers to be spun into yarn and woven into fabric. Most wool comes from sheep and goats, but wool is also taken from camels, llamas, and some breeds of rabbit. Due to a complex natural crimp, wool is extremely wrinkle resistant and returns to shape even after being stretched up to 20%.


Common items used with this: winter accessories such as hats and scarves, socks, sweaters
